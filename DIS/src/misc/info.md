Session outline:
The most difficult part of deploying Agentic Al is not the Al itself. In this talk we share experiences from the front lines of Agentic Al deployment to illuminate the critical steps all organizations must take to be Al-ready. We will specifically focus on the critical success factors for creation of Data Products aligned to decentralized governance models and the use of Al for enabling construction of a Knowledge Fabric. We will also highlight how to avoid common traps in deployment with a future-proofed approach leveraging an architected use of LLMs.
